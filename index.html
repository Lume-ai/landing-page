Our story

Built to eliminate the bottleneck between software teams and their customers' data.

We built Lume because customer integration was broken. Software teams were spending days, weeks, or months wrestling with legacy ERPs, custom databases, and messy schemas just to onboard a single new customer. AI handled schema discovery, suggested intelligent data mappings, validated data quality, and generated dbt code automatically - turning a manual process into a smooth, speedy experience.

We're proud of what we shipped and grateful for our customers who believed in it. To the engineering teams who trusted us with their integrations, the investors who backed the mission early, and every teammate who helped build it - thank you. The Lume chapter is closed, but we are joining Harvey to keep pushing on the harder problem: automating the complex, high-stakes work that still takes too long in professional workflows.

We owe a special thanks to our partners at Y Combinator, General Catalyst, Khosla Ventures, Floodgate, Soma Capital, and more, along with our angel investors, for believing in us from the beginning and for their guidance throughout this journey.
