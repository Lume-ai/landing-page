# Cardinal Gray Accelerated Implementation Timelines and Time-to-Value by 13x with Lume AI

Cardinal Gray needed to create mappings from customers' Loan Management Systems (LMS) to hundreds of internal and external data models representing standardized DMV titling schemas. The incoming data - whether CSV files, XML feeds, or database exports - had to be normalized and then transformed into standardized data models required for compliance titling documents. The complexity of mapping such diverse data sources to standardized schemas was a significant bottleneck. Cardinal Gray turned to Lume to build a scalable, automated mapping infrastructure that dramatically reduced time-to-market.

## Challenge

Mapping lender data from customer databases (LMS) to Cardinal Gray's selected standardized data models was a complex, error-prone process:

### Diverse Input Formats

Data arrived via CSV, XML, or database exports from various customer Loan Management Systems, each with its own structure.

### Complex Output Requirements

The common data models weren't a traditional database model, they represented compliance documents downstream, each following specific schemas with unique fields that needed precise mapping.

### Manual Mapping Overload

Engineering teams were spending weeks creating and maintaining custom mappers for every new integration, leading to delays and inefficiencies.

### Time-to-Market Pressure

The manual process led to larger implementation time for new customers, limiting the ability to onboard new lenders and adapt quickly to evolving compliance standards.

## Solution

Cardinal Gray implemented Lume's AI-powered mapping platform to automate and scale their customer data implementation process:

### Automated Mapping Pipelines

Lume's platform automatically harmonized input data from CSVs, XML, and database exports, transforming it into the standardized schemas needed for DMV titling.

### Dynamic Pipeline Management

Lume built and managed thousands of mapping pipelines that continuously updated as either the LMS source data or the PDF schemas evolved.

### Scalable Infrastructure

With Lume, manual intervention was minimized, enabling rapid onboarding of new lenders and supporting hundreds of DMV documents without a proportional increase in engineering work.

### End-to-End Workflow

Once the data was mapped to the required PDF fields, Cardinal Gray extracted the mapped data and filled out their downstream PDFs.
